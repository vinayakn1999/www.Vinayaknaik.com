Financial Data Analysis Project

Analyzing financial data is crucial for making informed decisions. In this project, I used Power BI to explore sales, revenue, costs, and profits to uncover actionable insights that can drive business growth and efficiency.

Objectives

  • Analyze key financial metrics (sales, profits, COGS).
  • Identify top-performing countries and products.
  • Understand trends over time and seasonal peaks.
  • Address challenges related to high costs and profitability.

Project Highlights

    • Total Sales: $127.93M | Revenue: $118.73M | COGS: $101.83M
    • COGS-to-Sales Ratio: 0.86 highlights high production costs impacting profits.
    • Top Regions: USA and Canada ($25M each), followed by France and Germany.
    • Sales Peaks: October ($22M) and December ($17M), suggesting strong seasonal influences.
    • Products like Paseo and VTT excelled, while others showed room for improvement.

Challenges Addressed

  • High COGS: Limited profit margins due to high costs.
  • Sales Seasonality: Peaks in October and December suggest opportunities for targeted promotions.
  • Regional Variation: Highlighted regions needing better strategies to boost sales.

Outcome

By leveraging Power BI, I transformed financial data into clear visualizations, uncovering insights that can drive decision-making and business efficiency.
